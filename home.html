      About This Application

      This GPA/CGPA Calculator is designed to help students easily calculate their GPA and CGPA based on their grades and credit hours. It provides a user-friendly interface to input subjects and their respective marks, making grade tracking simple and efficient.

      ___________________________________________________

      Features

      • Calculate GPA and CGPA with ease.
      • Save records of your GPA and CGPA for future reference.
      • Toggle between light and dark modes for better visibility.
      • Choose between different grading scales (4.0 and 5.0).
